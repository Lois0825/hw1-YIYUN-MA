# About Me and My Interests

Hi, I’m currently a master’s student in Information Systems and AI for Business. My background is not purely technical, so I’ve been actively learning programming and data-related skills during this program. I’m especially interested in how these skills can be applied in real business scenarios, not just theory.

One area I find really interesting is using AI to support business decisions. From what I’ve seen, many companies collect a lot of data, but it’s not always used effectively. I think AI has the potential to bridge that gap by making data more understandable and actionable, especially for non-technical users.

Here are three ways generative AI can be useful in this area:
- Generating quick summaries or reports from large datasets, which saves time
- Providing explanations of trends so decision-makers can understand insights more easily
- Helping build simple analytical tools or dashboards with less manual coding
